About NES

The National Entrepreneurship Summit 2020 is an initiative by E-Cell, NIT Rourkela to bridge the gap between students mind and his dream to become an entrepreneur. Formerly known as Vritantta , NES will be a platform for learning & interaction of students, entrepreneurs, faculties, industry leaders etc. from across the World. It shall be a mix of Key Note Speakers, Guest Lectures, Events, Workshops, Video Conference, Start-Up Camp, Panel Discussion and Round Table Conference.
