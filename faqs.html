NIT Rourkela is situated in Sector 2 of Rourkela, Odisha. Rourkela is accessible by rail and road. By train, one may arrive at the Rourkela Station. If you are arriving by bus, get off at the Sector 2 bus stop. Auto-rickshaw is also available. The prices are about Rs 120 from railway station and Rs 80 from Sector 2.

The campus has 5 restaurants, 3 situated near the hostel areas and 2 within the academic area. Amul has a store that provides snacks too. Near the hostel area you can find a stationary shop that will provide all the stationary related materials. Within the institute area you can find a student shop and a book store. The hostels have day and night canteens as well. The campus has four SBI ATMs, one ICICI ATM and one AXIS bank ATM within the institute.
